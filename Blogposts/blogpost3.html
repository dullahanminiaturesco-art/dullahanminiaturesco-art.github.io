By Camden Bohanan · October 28, 2025 ·

To begin the project proposal that I aim continuing to follow through on is to create a set of 4 men-at-artms models that can be used for either wargames or tabletop-rpg systems. The models will take some inspiration from house blackwood of a song of ice and fire for their theme or tone. As the current research unfolds I currently use the official song of ice and fire miniatures for the miniatures game as a basis on where I should lead the design of the men-at-arms. So far I've decided to take a more rustic/rugged approach to their design. I plan to also have them wear a form of kettle helm mainly to the fact it reminds of straw hats which can lead to them looking like scarecrows which in a way fits thematically since one of the symbols for house blackwood is a raven. For the other segments of the armor I plan to keep them simple to keep the rugged look to them. As of now I am currently creating the concept art for the miniatures.
